My Background


I’m Brandon Renschler, founder of Renschler Rentals LLC. I specialize in rental arbitrage—leasing properties from landlords, furnishing them, and operating them as high-performing short-term rentals designed to generate consistent monthly income.

My background is in cybersecurity, security engineering, and data analytics. I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Cybersecurity Analytics & Operations from Penn State (3.99 GPA) and am CompTIA Security+ certified.

I’ve worked in security operations, incident response, and threat intelligence roles where I developed strong systems thinking, automation, and risk management skills. That same structured, analytical approach is what I now apply to managing rental operations, pricing systems, and guest experience workflows.

Across my experience in both cybersecurity and data analytics, I’ve built and worked with automation systems, dashboards, and operational processes designed to improve efficiency, reduce risk, and scale performance—skills that directly translate into running short-term rentals as structured, data-driven assets.

My focus with Renschler Rentals is simple: reduce landlord vacancy risk, maintain property condition, and turn real estate into a predictable, hands-off income stream through systems, automation, and consistent execution.
